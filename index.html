iPhone Screen Sharing Instructions

To share your iPhone screen:

  1. Make sure you're using Safari browser
  2. Click "Share Screen" below
  3. Tap "Screen" in the share menu
  4. Select "Start Broadcast" when prompted

Note: Screen sharing on iOS requires iOS 15+ and Safari.

How to Share Your iPhone Screen

Follow these steps to share your iPhone screen:

  1. When prompted, select "Screen" from the share options
  2. Toggle on "Broadcast Setup" from control center if shown
  3. Tap "Start Broadcast" when ready
  4. Your screen will now be shared with the extension

Important: Do not leave Safari during screen sharing or the connection will be lost.
